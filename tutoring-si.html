Tutoring + Supplemental Instruction (SI)

Explore how VR Tutoring and Supplemental Instruction with Curio XR is transforming the learning experience at NYIT.

What is VR Tutoring?

+

VR Tutoring through Curio XR offers a cutting-edge approach to personalized learning. It immerses students in a distraction-free virtual environment designed to maximize engagement and retention. By combining VR technology with proven tutoring techniques, students can explore, practice, and understand concepts more effectively.

  • 3D interactive models for real-world simulation and problem-solving.
  • Collaborative tools for group study and SI sessions in virtual classrooms.
  • Focus-enhancing, distraction-free environments tailored to individual needs.

What is Supplemental Instruction (SI)?

+

Supplemental Instruction (SI) provides peer-led study sessions that focus on challenging courses and topics. With Curio XR, SI becomes even more impactful by leveraging VR to create an interactive and collaborative environment for students to work together.

  • Real-time problem-solving with peers in a shared virtual space.
  • Guided exploration of complex topics using 3D simulations.
  • Interactive discussion boards and virtual whiteboards for brainstorming.

Benefits of VR Tutoring and SI

+

By combining VR tutoring with Supplemental Instruction, students gain a more dynamic, hands-on learning experience. Here are some of the top benefits:

  • Enhanced Engagement: Interactive models and simulations keep students actively involved.
  • Better Retention: Visual and kinesthetic learning methods improve memory and understanding.
  • Inclusivity: Tailored VR environments ensure accessibility for all learners, including those with disabilities.
  • Collaboration: Virtual classrooms enable seamless group study and peer-to-peer interactions.
