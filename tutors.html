Info for Tutors

Discover how NYIT's innovative VR Ready Program empowers tutors and students to excel in a virtual reality environment.

Understanding the VR Ready Program

+

The VR Ready Program is designed to prepare tutors for virtual reality-based tutoring sessions. By joining the program, tutors will develop advanced skills to leverage Curio XR’s cutting-edge technology effectively.

This program focuses on:

  • Mastering VR hand gestures and tool interactions.
  • Utilizing 3D learning models for interactive teaching.
  • Accommodating diverse learning styles and accessibility needs.

Graduates of the VR Ready Program earn digital badges, showcasing their expertise and commitment to innovative teaching methods.

How to Get VR Ready

+

Becoming VR Ready involves completing two key steps:

  1. Book an Appointment: Schedule your VR Ready session by contacting the Learning Center. You’ll receive guidance on the required equipment and initial setup.
  2. Complete Curio XR Training: Engage in hands-on training that covers VR navigation, tools, and best practices. Tutors will complete interactive sessions to ensure mastery of Curio XR features.

By completing this process, tutors will gain the skills needed to create engaging, distraction-free learning environments for their students.
