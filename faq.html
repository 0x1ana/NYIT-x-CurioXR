Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Find answers to the most common questions about VR Tutoring and Curio XR.

1. What is VR Tutoring?

+

VR Tutoring uses virtual reality to create an immersive, distraction-free environment for learning. Students can engage with interactive 3D models, simulations, and collaborative tools to enhance their understanding.

2. What equipment do I need for VR Tutoring?

+

You’ll need access to a VR headset (MetaQuest 2 or 3) compatible with Curio XR. If you don’t have one, the learning center at NYIT can provide you with the headset. In order to book a VR tutoring appointment or come by and test out the app, please email to learningcenterma@nyit.edu.

3. How do I book a VR Tutoring session?

+

To book a session, contact the NYIT Learning Center via email: learningcenterma@nyit.edu or visit us in person. Our office is located on the second floor of 26W & 61st St. Room number is 205. Once you contact us, you’ll receive instructions on preparing for your session and accessing the Curio XR platform.

4. Is VR Tutoring free for NYIT students?

+

Yes, VR Tutoring is completely free for all NYIT students. The program is supported by grants and partnerships with Curio XR.

5. Can I join VR Tutoring from home?

+

Yes, if you have a compatible VR headset and access to a stable internet connection, you can join VR Tutoring sessions from home.

6. How does VR Tutoring compare to traditional tutoring?

+

VR Tutoring offers an immersive experience that allows students to engage with 3D models and simulations. It is especially effective for hands-on learning and visualizing complex concepts, which can be harder to achieve in traditional tutoring.

7. Can I request a specific topic for a VR Tutoring session?

+

Yes, you can request specific topics when booking your session. Tutors will customize the VR experience to meet your learning needs.

8. What happens during a Supplemental Instruction (SI) session?

+

During an SI session, students collaborate in a virtual environment to solve problems, discuss challenging topics, and explore concepts using interactive tools. SI leaders guide these sessions to maximize learning.

9. Are there accessibility features available in VR Tutoring?

+

Yes, Curio XR is designed to be accessible to all learners. Features include customizable environments, support for various learning styles, and tools for students with disabilities.

10. How do I provide feedback on VR Tutoring?

+

Feedback is encouraged and essential for improving the program. Use the feedback form to share your thoughts or suggest new features.
