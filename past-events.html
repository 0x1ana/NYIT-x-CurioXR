Past Events

Explore highlights from our recent events showcasing the power of VR Tutoring and Curio XR.

VR Tutoring Launch Event

The VR Tutoring Launch Event marked the beginning of a new era in education at NYIT, introducing students and tutors to the transformative potential of virtual reality. Attendees explored immersive learning environments powered by CurioXR, experiencing firsthand how VR enhances engagement and retention in tutoring sessions.

Event Highlights:

  • Hands-on VR demonstrations showcasing CurioXR’s features.
  • Interactive workshops for students and tutors to explore virtual tools.

This groundbreaking event demonstrated the incredible possibilities of VR in education, leaving participants inspired and excited for the future of learning.

CurioXR Demonstration with PSG for Freshmen

The CurioXR Demonstration with PSG was a collaborative event aimed at introducing freshmen to the transformative possibilities of virtual reality in education. Hosted in partnership with the Peer to Success Group (PSG), the event provided an engaging introduction to NYIT’s cutting-edge VR tutoring platform.

Event Highlights:

  • Freshmen explored CurioXR’s features, including interactive 3D models and immersive simulations.
  • PSG leaders shared insights into how CurioXR supports student success and enhances learning outcomes.
  • Participants engaged in hands-on VR experiences, discovering how virtual environments make studying more interactive and enjoyable.

This event successfully equipped incoming students with the knowledge and tools to leverage VR technology, fostering excitement for their academic journey at NYIT.

BioReality: Discover Biology Through CurioXR

BioReality: Discover Biology Through CurioXR was an immersive event that showcased the future of biology education through CurioXR. Students explored complex biological concepts in a highly interactive and engaging virtual reality environment. From virtual dissections to 3D neuron simulations, BioReality transformed traditional biology learning into a cutting-edge experience.

Highlights:

  • Students performed virtual dissections with precision tools.
  • Participants explored the human body using detailed 3D models.
  • Complex biological processes were explained through interactive VR simulations.

This event empowered students to deepen their understanding of biology, making learning both accessible and unforgettable.
